# Poker Sense

An AI-powered No-Limit Texas Hold'em training app for iOS.

## About

Poker Sense helps you sharpen your poker skills through practice and analysis. Play cash games and Sit & Go tournaments against intelligent AI opponents, then review your play with premium coaching tools that identify leaks and help you improve.

## Features

### AI Opponents
Challenge yourself against three difficulty levels:
- **Beginner (L1)** — Straightforward play, perfect for learning fundamentals
- **Intermediate (L2)** — Position-aware with fold equity and equity-based decisions
- **Advanced (L3)** — Balanced strategies with blocker awareness, MDF guardrails, and ICM adjustments (Premium)

### Game Modes
- **Cash Games** — 2–8 player No-Limit Hold'em with configurable blinds and buy-ins
- **Sit & Go Tournaments** — Single-table tournaments with escalating blinds, eliminations, and payouts

### Premium Coaching
Unlock advanced analysis tools to accelerate your improvement:
- **EV Analysis** — See expected value calculations for every decision
- **Sizing Curves** — Visualize optimal bet sizing across scenarios
- **ICM Analysis** — Understand tournament equity and bubble dynamics
- **Leak Detection** — Identify patterns and weaknesses in your play
- **Training Drills** — Targeted exercises to fix specific leaks
- **Hand Review** — Detailed post-session hand-by-hand breakdowns

### Fully Offline
All data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

### Play-Money Only
Educational tool using play-money chips with no real-world cash value. Not a gambling app.
